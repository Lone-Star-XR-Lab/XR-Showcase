Hardware — Standalone VR • PCVR • AR (Glasses / Phone / Other)

Standalone VR

  • All-in-one (no PC required).
  • Color passthrough → mixed reality.

PCVR

  • Desktop-GPU fidelity & mods.
  • Great for simulation & CAD.

AR Glasses

  • Heads-up overlays, hands-free.
  • Prompts, captions, diagrams.

Phone / Tablet AR

  • No headset needed (iOS/Android).
  • Plane & image tracking via camera.

Other AR Surfaces

  • HUDs, kiosks, smart displays.
  • Projection mapping & wayfinding.
