Standalone Headset (VR/MR)

  • Runs apps with no PC; fast to set up.
  • Color passthrough for mixed reality.
  • Hand tracking + controllers.

Control: Hands & Controllers

  • Pinch/tap gestures for menus & tools.
  • Precise controllers for painting & surgery sims.
  • Voice input in many apps.

Passthrough & Spatial Anchors

  • Blend 3D models into your real room.
  • Pin content to desks, walls, and floors.
  • Safer, more social demos.

AR Glasses (Viewer)

  • Lightweight displays powered by phone/PC.
  • Great for heads-up info & media.
  • Emerging classroom use.

PC VR (Optional)

  • Tethers to PC for high-end visuals.
  • Used for advanced sims & CAD.
  • Less portable than standalone.

Extras

  • Prescription inserts & spacers for glasses.
  • Battery straps for longer sessions.
  • Face gaskets for comfort & hygiene.
