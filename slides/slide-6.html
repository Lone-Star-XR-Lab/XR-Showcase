Comfort, Accessibility & Safety — built in

Comfort

  • Seated or room-scale; instant re-center.
  • Teleport / step locomotion; snap-turn options.
  • Vignette (tunnel) for smooth-motion comfort.
  • Short 10–15 min segments; easy pause/resume.
  • Passthrough “peek” to the real room when needed.

Accessibility

  • Subtitles & captions where supported.
  • High-contrast, large UI; color-safe palettes.
  • Hand-tracking; one-hand / limited-mobility modes.
  • Voice commands; remappable controls.
  • Haptics + audio cues for non-visual feedback.

Safety

  • Guardian boundaries; clear floor space.
  • IPD fit & eyewear spacer for glasses wearers.
  • Avoid intense artificial acceleration; start gentle.
  • Comfort ratings & gradual ramp-up for new users.
  • Regular breaks; good lighting; hydrate.
