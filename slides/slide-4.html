Beyond class: empathy & life skills

VR can place learners in perspectives and situations they can't otherwise access—building understanding and decision-making.

  • • Autismity / In the Shoes of an Autistic Person
  • • Conflict resolution & de-escalation practice
  • • Diversity, equity, inclusion scenarios
  • • Emergency response & preparedness

Quick demo structure

  1. 90-second empathy clip
  2. Hands-on task (e.g., CPR hand placement)
  3. Guided field trip in MR (blend real room + model)

Keep each demo under 2–3 minutes to fit group sessions.

If time is short, run just the empathy clip and one task demo.
